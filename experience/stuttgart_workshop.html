Stuttgart IAS Workshop

This was a workshop between the GUC and University of Stuttgart titled "Cooperation of Heterogenous Agents in Industrial Application".

Workshop Objective

The workshop focused on the integration of seamless collaborative operations when performing sequential object handovers between three different robots: a TurtleBot, a Unitree Go1, and a Franka Emika Panda. Firstly, Gazebo simulations of all three robots were conducted and different algorithms for mapping, localization, path planning, and pick and place were tested. After verification of the algorithms, we started deploying and testing our algorithms on the hardware iteratively until each independent system was fully operational. Lastly, a socket server was developed to allow for communication between the three robots.

This ambitious project lasted two weeks under the supervision of Jun.-Prof. Andrey Morozov and PhD candidates M.Sc. Philipp Grimmeisen and M.Sc. Georgios Katranis.
