Design and Manufacturing Engineer

Jetsolar

I started my career with JetSolar as a Design and Manufacturing Engineer, developing Egypt's first PWRPOD, a solar-powered silent pod with the aim of enhancing public workspaces and boosting urban mobility.

Duties

  • Leading the design of PWRPOD, Egypt's first hybrid-powered insulated work pod designed to enhance public workspaces
  • Supervising and assisting with a number of manufacturing processes
  • Researching and implementing several features including hybrid power and QR access
  • Testing the product in real life scenarios and recording limitations

Design

This is one of several prototypes for the design of the PWRPOD. It involved numerous components and complex geometries to create a visually stunning pod. I designed and assembled the entire prototype in SolidWorks. The pod includes several parts including
  • Metal frame
  • Rotating glass door
  • Wooden desk with wireless charging
  • Solar panels and their mounts
  • Internal electrical components
  • QR code scanner and camera surveillance system

Manufacturing

Manufacturing the PWRPOD was a siginificantly challenging process. A lot of conventional tools were either not available or incredibly difficult to source in Egypt. The team and I had to get really creative about how we produce the prototype in reasonable time with superb quality. Some of the processes required included

  • CNC routing
  • CNC laser machining
  • Sheet metal rolling and bending

With the effort of the team, the technicians, and myself, we were able to create an initial presentable prototype within 2 months to include the most critical features of the pod. This allowed us to start marketing for the product and progress further with the proposed business plan.
