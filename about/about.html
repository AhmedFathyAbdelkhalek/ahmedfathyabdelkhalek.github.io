About me

Hi, I'm Ahmed. I'm a Mechatronics Engineer dedicated to bridging the gap between theoretical robotics and industrial application.

My journey began at the German University in Cairo, leading to an MSc in Engineering and Materials Science, where I specialized in designing compliant robotic harvesters. My research is driven by precision and innovation, recognized through a 3rd Best Paper Award and publications at international conferences like CCRIS and NILES.

Currently, I apply this technical depth as the Design Engineer Team Lead at FabriHub. Beyond just creating CAD models, I manage the entire design-to-quote pipeline and lead a team of engineers to solve complex manufacturing challenges. I also bring hands-on experience from developing automated KUKA robotic solutions and teaching the next generation of engineers.

When I'm not leading a design review or fine-tuning a robot, you can find me playing competitive table tennis or enjoying some video games. If you would like to connect, please feel free to reach out.
